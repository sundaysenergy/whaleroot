HUG A POLAR BEAR
In my career I've gravitated toward working for clients and initiatives with environmentally-sustainable and socially-conscious values. Using my tools of design to advocate these types of policies is a big part of my practice—it makes me feel all warm and squishy inside. Design is great but, making a positive impact while doing design is pure awesome!

Every project I do has the starting goal to generate the greatest positive impact while staying carbon neutral for the viewer, the client, and the Earth. Granted, not every project works out that way but, you can't win 'em all. The important thing is that a sincere effort is made—and when that effort pays off, it's just delightful.

Simply put: Love the Earth and it'll love you back.
