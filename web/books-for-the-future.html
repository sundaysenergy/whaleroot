    CLICK THE CLICKY
    Technology is all around us nowadays! I got twenty dollars that says even your grandma has a website by now. Or at least a blog.

    I still consider myself a very analog designer—with print as my main medium—but, web design is a big part of what I do, as well. If design is about making ideas accessible to the masses then there's really no better way to do that than make something awesome and throwing it on the World Wide Web.

    To keep from sensory overload, though, my designs are usually strikingly clean and simple. I like to get by with smartly written HTML, CSS, PHP, and—sometimes—just a dash of JavaScript. However, I'm not opposed to incorporating Flash elements for a little interactive pizzaz. Either way, guaranteed your mouse is gonna go bananas.
