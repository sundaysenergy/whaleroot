By way of Seattle and Minneapolis, I live and work in Chicago under the moniker, Whaleroot.

A lot of the work I do is in the environmental, nonprofit, educational, and social-service fields but—if you boil it down—I just love to design. I have a knack for cleanly handling typography, implementing strong systems, being intensely organized, and for putting the concept front and center. All of this feeds into a style that seems to be well-received by the various clients and collaborators I've had the pleasure of working with over the years.

My passion for print and identity design is borderline alarming and I'm always interested in new opportunities. So contact me today—I would love to meet you.

I also love sharks. But that's a different story.
