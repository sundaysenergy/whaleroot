About us:

America Home Lending Mortgage, Inc. was formed and founded in August of 1998 by Olivia E.C. Townsend. Olivia started in the industry in 1993 working as a loan officer and then working her way up the ranks before breaking off on her own as the President of America Home Lending Mortgage, Inc.

Our number one goal at America Home Lending Mortgage, Inc. is to find you a loan that is specific to your needs. Whether you are self-employed or use W-2's, have good credit or bad credit or are a first time homebuyer. We can offer Zero down, 100% financing, no income verification, fixed and adjustable rate mortgages, full documentation for low rates, and many more. We can also close loans in multiple states.

Excellent customer service, experienced loan originators, and a diverse and bilingual staff are what keep our clients coming back and referring us to their friends, neighbors, and co-workers. At America Home Lending Mortgage, Inc. we can proudly say we work on a 100% referral basis.
